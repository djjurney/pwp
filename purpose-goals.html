Purpose, Audience & Goals

Purpose: 

The PWP will serve as my Professional Website. It will have a portfolio and highlight my coding skills. It will also have a resume add.

Audience: 

This will be designed for a professional audience looking for Fullstack back-end developers. Specifically targeting employment opportunities and a means to connect with other developers.

Goals

Primary goals for the PWP will be to find employment opportunities, show my portfolio and resume. Secondary goals with be to connect with other developers and collaboration opportunities.
